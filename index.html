Motivations


Learn2Surf is a capstone project started by a group of 5 students.

We desired to create educational content that would help teach students

about the dangers of the internet, and the responsibilities that come with browsing it.

When we started out, we felt that there was a distinct lack of adaquate educational

content for teachers to use in their classrooms. With the danger of malicious

actors out in the wild and their increasing prevelence, we felt like we needed

to take the reigns into our own hands. So we decided to prepare educational content

that can be consumed by teachers to help educate their students on those dangers

and how they can protect themselves.
